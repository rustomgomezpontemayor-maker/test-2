About This Project

The final defense is the concluding stage of the research project where the proponents present and explain their completed study, the Guyabano Lowering System Device, before a panel of evaluators.

The presentation includes the project's objectives, design, methodology, functionality, results, and significance. It also serves as an opportunity to demonstrate how the device helps improve the safe and efficient harvesting of guyabano fruits while answering questions and recommendations from the panel.
